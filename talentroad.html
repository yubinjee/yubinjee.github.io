Talentroad

Great talents deserve great companies

Year

2019

Team

Yu Bin Jee

Roland Jee

Role

Branding, UI

Talentroad is a global IT scout that helps great talents and companies come together. They handpick top global IT talents and ensure a higher likelihood of hiring success with less pain.

Our job was creating the brand identity as well as designing and developing the website. The client asked for an exceptional, fancy but professional visual concept to differentiate from their competitors (i.e. other recruiting companies). They wanted to appear modern and contemporary, but with humanity playing a major role at the same time. The client also wished for a logo in a decent relation to the "road" of the company's name.
