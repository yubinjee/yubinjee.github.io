Talentroad

Great talents deserve great companies

Talentroad is a global IT scout that helps great talents and companies come together. They handpick top global IT talents and ensure a higher likelihood of hiring success with less pain.

Our job was creating the brand identity, as well as designing and developing the website. My main responsibillity was the design part. The client asked for exceptional, fancy and professional visual concept to differentiate from their competitors (i.e. other recruiting companies). They wanted to appear modern and contemporary, but with humanity playing a major role.

As for the company's name, the client wished to have a logo with decent road character which is not too obvious.

The work isn’t disclosed to the public yet.
