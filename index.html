Hi, I’m Yu Bin.
I do design and build digital products.

I’m a UI designer based in Berlin and who loves building things that live on the internet.
I focus on three things: Figma, HTML and CSS.

About me

I am currently working as a freelance designer and am always interested in a challenge.
I discovered my curiosity for design when I was 10 years old and coincidentally found Adobe Photoshop 4.0 on my father's laptop.

Experience

I am a self-taught designer and developer. My first professional job was in a small design agency Netzschritt in Heidelberg where I worked as a graphic illustrator at the age of 16.

After that, I devoted myself to my other passion, fashion, and worked in various fashion companies like Zalando. During my fashion design degree at the University of Applied Sciences in Berlin, I worked in a sustainable fashion online shop Loveco as a UI designer and frontend developer where I was able to combine my two favorite topics, fashion and digital products.

At the moment I am working on smaller side projects in UX / UI design, graphic design and frontend development.
