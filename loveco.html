Role

Branding, UI, frontend development

Loveo is an online shop for fair fashion and green lifestyle. The shopware-based shop was already operating some years but was urgently in need of a visual refresh. Loveco wanted a fundamental change in their digital appearance and a new visual concept.

Our job was to re-create the brand identity while keeping some of the old visual components (i.e. logo), as well as re-designing and developing the frontend of the online shop.

Mostly I designed and built many custom modules for Shopware content management system while using HTML, CSS (LESS) and some basic JS and PHP (Smarty). The modules could be used by content editors for creating i.e. landingpages to advance marketing campaigns with greater flexibility for their content.

* Notice: Since leaving, other people took over the project and the live shop won't appear like shown in the visualizations anymore.
